Best Shows on Netflix

1. The Office



The Office in my opinion is the funniest show on Netflix. It's full of insane, idiotic characters doing a terrible job attempting to work ethically in the workplace. What's special about this show is that it never gets old.
It possesses die-hard fans, including myself, who have watched the show over 10 times


2. Stranger Things



Stranger Things is a popular Netflix Original and Thriller show. 5 kids are trying to save the town from monsters,
but the hard part is that few people in the town are aware of these monsters' existance.


3. Narcos



Narcos is also a Netflix Original and arguably gives Breaking Bad a run for it's money. It depicts the life of the notorious drug lord
Pablo Escobar and his reign over Columbia.


3. Orange is the New Black



Several female inmates find themselves in prison after making wrong decisions. Many of these mischevious inmates are
up to no good, yet they eventually come together as a family while wreaking havoc within the prison.
